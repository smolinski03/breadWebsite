Banana Bread

They've put a banana in my bun, and it works! This delicious recipe is a sweet alternative to conventional bread we all love.

ingredients

  1. flour
  2. butter
  3. yogurt
  4. brown sugar
  5. bananas (overripe)
  6. vanilla
  7. baking powder
  8. egg
  9. baking soda

steps

  1. Mash bananas and mix with yogurt and baking soda in a small bowl.
  2. Combine soft butter, brown sugar, egg and vanilla in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients just until combined. Donâ€™t over-mix.
  4. egg
  5. Spoon batter into parchment-lined loaf pan.
  6. Bake until the tester comes out clean. Cool to room temperature.
